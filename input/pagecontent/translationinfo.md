# Connectathon feedback

We welcome your feedback during the connectathon! Please share your experience, issues, and successes.

## How to provide feedback

Use our [Connectathon Feedback Document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1PdQ8zBI9xkISP3tAqIK8-TGMql3kVVZ4UNoHVYqCy4Y/edit?usp=sharing) to:

- Report issues with profiles and implementation guides
- Share feedback on the API functionality
- Document your successes with screenshots and recordings
- Ask questions and request clarifications

Your feedback helps us improve the Digital Health Platform for Uzbekistan.
